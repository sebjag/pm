8. Take Steps to Fix Your Mistakes
(15 min.) Discussion. 8:37

Picture description: Collage: Scenes from the video “My Teen Life​—How Can I Fix My Mistakes?” 1. Thalila. 2. José.
 
No matter how hard we try, we all make mistakes. (1Jo 1:8) When we do, we should never let embarrassment or fear of getting into trouble prevent us from turning to Jehovah for forgiveness and help. (1Jo 1:9) Turning to Jehovah is always the first step in fixing a mistake.
Read Psalm 51:1, 2, 17. Then ask the audience:
How can David’s words motivate us to turn to Jehovah for help if we make a serious mistake?
Play the VIDEO My Teen Life​—How Can I Fix My Mistakes? Then ask the audience:
What are some things that led up to Thalila and José making mistakes?
What steps did they take to fix their mistakes?
How did they benefit from doing so?
