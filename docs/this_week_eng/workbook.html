7. How Can We Loyally Support the Kingdom?
(15 min.) Discussion. 8:37

Picture description: A scene from the video “Loyally Support the ‘Prince of Peace.’” Two brothers in the ministry talk with a man at a fishing harbor.
 
Jehovah’s Kingdom government is an expression of his universal sovereignty. (Da 2:44, 45) Therefore, when we actively support God’s Kingdom, we are upholding Jehovah’s sovereignty.
Play the VIDEO Loyally Support the “Prince of Peace.” Then ask the audience:
How do we loyally support God’s Kingdom?
Write down a scripture that corresponds to each of the following ways of supporting God’s Kingdom.
Making the Kingdom our most important concern in life.
Adhering to the moral standards required of Kingdom subjects.
Zealously telling others about the Kingdom.
Showing respect for secular governments, but obeying God when there is a conflict between Caesar’s law and God’s law.
